## 1. Procedure: Sync
1. Copy the full content of `scripts/logic_payload.js` from the canvas.
2. Paste it into your local `scripts/logic_payload.txt`.
3. **IMPORTANT**: DO NOT perform any Find & Replace operations. The protocol is now "Zero-Touch". Braces are handled by the patcher.

1. Save the file.
